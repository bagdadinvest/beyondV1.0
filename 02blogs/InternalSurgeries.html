Internal Surgeries

A second chance 

by Christian M. Xavier — Posted in Society on May 19, 2014

Internal surgeries, also known as invasive surgeries, involve procedures that require an incision or entry into the body. These surgeries are performed to repair damage, remove diseased tissues, or correct abnormalities.

In Turkey, internal surgeries are performed using advanced medical technologies and techniques. The country has a strong healthcare system with well-trained surgeons and modern facilities. Patients from around the world visit Turkey for various types of internal surgeries, attracted by the high standards of care and the relatively lower costs compared to other countries.

Common types of internal surgeries performed in Turkey include cardiovascular surgeries, gastrointestinal surgeries, and orthopedic surgeries. These procedures are carried out in state-of-the-art hospitals equipped with the latest medical equipment.

Cardiovascular surgeries in Turkey include procedures such as coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG), heart valve repair or replacement, and aortic aneurysm repair. These surgeries are crucial for patients with heart diseases and are performed by experienced cardiac surgeons.

Gastrointestinal surgeries address issues related to the digestive system. Procedures such as gastric bypass, colectomy, and liver resection are commonly performed. These surgeries help in treating conditions like obesity, colorectal cancer, and liver diseases.

Orthopedic surgeries focus on the musculoskeletal system. Common procedures include joint replacements, spine surgeries, and fracture repairs. These surgeries help patients regain mobility and reduce pain caused by injuries or degenerative conditions.

Cardiovascular Surgeries

Turkey is renowned for its expertise in cardiovascular surgeries. The country's hospitals are equipped with advanced cardiac care units and surgical theaters. Surgeons use minimally invasive techniques whenever possible, reducing recovery times and improving outcomes for patients.

Patients undergoing cardiovascular surgeries in Turkey benefit from comprehensive care, including pre-operative evaluations, surgical interventions, and post-operative rehabilitation. The multidisciplinary approach ensures that patients receive the best possible treatment and support throughout their recovery.

Gastrointestinal Surgeries

Gastrointestinal surgeries in Turkey are performed by skilled surgeons who specialize in digestive health. The procedures are designed to treat a wide range of conditions, from benign disorders to complex cancers. The use of laparoscopic and robotic-assisted techniques allows for precise and less invasive surgeries.

Patients receive personalized care plans tailored to their specific needs. This includes dietary counseling, physical therapy, and follow-up care to ensure a smooth recovery. The focus is on improving the patient's quality of life and managing any underlying health issues.

Orthopedic Surgeries

Orthopedic surgeries in Turkey are performed by experienced surgeons who specialize in treating musculoskeletal disorders. The country's hospitals are equipped with advanced imaging and surgical technologies, enabling accurate diagnoses and effective treatments.

Common orthopedic procedures include hip and knee replacements, spinal fusions, and arthroscopic surgeries. These surgeries help patients regain function and alleviate pain caused by injuries, arthritis, and other conditions. Post-operative care includes physical therapy and rehabilitation programs to support recovery.

High Standards of Care

Turkey's healthcare system is known for its high standards of care. The country's hospitals are accredited by international organizations, ensuring that they meet stringent quality and safety criteria. Patients can expect to receive top-notch medical care from highly qualified healthcare professionals.

Medical tourism is a growing industry in Turkey, with many hospitals offering specialized services for international patients. These services include assistance with travel arrangements, accommodation, and language support. The goal is to provide a seamless and comfortable experience for patients seeking medical treatment in Turkey.

In conclusion, Turkey is a leading destination for internal surgeries, offering advanced medical treatments at competitive prices. Patients benefit from the expertise of skilled surgeons, modern facilities, and comprehensive care plans. Whether it's cardiovascular, gastrointestinal, or orthopedic surgery, Turkey provides high-quality medical care that meets international standards.
